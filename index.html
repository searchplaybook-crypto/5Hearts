Our Story

At Five Hearts Events Decor & Hire, we believe that every celebration tells a story and every beautiful event becomes a treasured memory.

Our business was born from the love of a mother and her four daughters, united by a shared passion for creating unforgettable moments for families - just like our own. What began as a love for decorating family celebrations has grown into a business dedicated to transforming life's special occasions into beautiful, lasting memories.

In 2025, our family experienced the heartbreaking loss of our beloved father. As we reflected on the countless birthdays, anniversaries, holidays, and family gatherings we shared with him, we realised something truly special: the love, care and attention we poured into those celebrations made those memories even more meaningful.

Inspired by those cherished memories, we founded Five Hearts with one purpose - to help other families create moments they will treasure for a lifetime. Our greatest inspiration is our mom, whose remarkable strength carried our family through one of life's most difficult seasons.

"We Decorate Memories. You Celebrate Life."

Questions

What People Ask.

Simply browse our collection, tap the WhatsApp button on any item, and send us your event date, venue, and the pieces you love. We reply within hours with availability, a personalised quote, and delivery options. No login, no cart - just a conversation.

We are based in Athlone and primarily serve Bridgetown, Athlone, and surrounding Cape Town areas. Delivery and collection can be arranged for most locations within the greater Cape Town region.

From July 2026 until July 2027, we are offering decor and event hire services only. You are welcome to style your own way or work with your preferred decorator using our curated collection.

There is no strict minimum - whether you need a single statement piece or a complete collection for 200 guests, we are here to help. Every enquiry is treated with the same care and attention to detail.

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for weddings and large events, and 2-3 weeks for smaller gatherings. Peak season (November-January) fills up quickly.

A refundable breakage deposit is payable with every booking, held separately from your rental fee. Once all items are returned and inspected, the full deposit is refunded within 7 business days if nothing is missing or damaged. If any items are lost, broken, stained, or need excessive cleaning, the reasonable repair or replacement cost is deducted from the deposit — you'll always be notified of any deduction with an itemised breakdown on request. Normal wear and tear is never charged. Full Terms & Conditions are shared with your quote before booking is confirmed.
